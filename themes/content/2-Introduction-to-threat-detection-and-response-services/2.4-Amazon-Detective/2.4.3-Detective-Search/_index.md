#### Detective - Search

1. Open the **Search** page in Detective.  
https://us-east-1.console.aws.amazon.com/detective/home?region=us-east-1#search  

2. As an example, search for the role you are currently using. For **Select type**, choose **AWS Role**. Enter **"WSParticipantRole"** and then click **Search**.

![VPC](/images/2/2.4/2.4.3/s2.png)

3. Select the **Principal ID** to view how the WSParticipantRole has been used. Take a moment to review the visualizations and tabs on this page. Note the scope time displayed in the top-right corner. This activity should reflect what you have been doing during the workshop.

![VPC](/images/2/2.4/2.4.3/s2b.png)
